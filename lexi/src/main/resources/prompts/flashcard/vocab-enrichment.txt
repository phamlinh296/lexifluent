VOCABULARY ENRICHMENT RULES — IELTS CONTEXT

For each vocabulary word, generate enriched content following these rules:

EXAMPLE SENTENCE:
  - Must be IELTS Writing Task 2 style (academic, formal)
  - Must naturally include a key collocation for the word
  - Length: 15–25 words
  ✓ "The detrimental effects of rapid urbanization on biodiversity cannot be overstated."
  ✗ "This is a detrimental situation for many people."

PHONETIC:
  - IPA transcription of the word's primary form
  ✓ /ˈdetɹɪməntl/

VIETNAMESE MEANING:
  - 1–4 words, accurate Vietnamese equivalent
  ✓ "có hại", "đáng kể", "quan trọng"
  ✗ Full translated sentence

DEFINITION:
  - Concise English-only definition (1 sentence max)
  ✓ "Causing harm or damage to something"
  ✗ Long dictionary paragraph

CEFR + IELTS BAND MAPPING:
  B1 → band 5.5–6.0
  B2 → band 6.0–7.0
  C1 → band 7.0–8.0
  C2 → band 8.0+

FORMALITY: default FORMAL for academic IELTS vocabulary.

AVOID:
  - Textbook examples ("She is very happy.")
  - Over-explaining obvious words
  - Vietnamese translation of the full sentence (definition only)
