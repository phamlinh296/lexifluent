## Target Band: 8.0

Evaluation priorities at this band:
- Expect near-native academic prose — errors should be isolated slips, not patterns
- Task Response: argument must be sophisticated, fully developed, and nuanced
- Coherence: essay should read as a coherent whole, not a series of paragraphs with transitions
- Vocabulary: wide range, precise, natural collocations; no word misuse
- Grammar: full range of structures; occasional non-systematic errors only

Scoring calibration for Band 8.0:
- Task Response: covers requirements sufficiently; presents, extends, and supports main ideas; clearly focused
- Coherence & Cohesion: sequences information and ideas logically; manages cohesion well; paragraphing is used appropriately
- Lexical Resource: uses a wide range of vocabulary fluently and flexibly; minor errors in word choice/spelling
- Grammatical Range & Accuracy: wide range of structures; majority of sentences error-free; only occasional slips

Focus for feedback:
- Identify any remaining formulaic expressions or template patterns — these cap the band
- Flag redundancy: saying the same thing twice in different words is a Band 8 ceiling
- Reward sophistication: subordination, fronting, cleft sentences used naturally
- Any vocabulary that sounds unnatural or overly formal for the context should be flagged
- Flag punctuation errors: comma splices, missing commas in non-restrictive clauses
