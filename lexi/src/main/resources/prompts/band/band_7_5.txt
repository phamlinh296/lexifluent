## Target Band: 7.5

Evaluation priorities at this band:
- This is a transitional band — the writer is close to Band 8 but has identifiable weaknesses
- Ideas must be fully developed with clear reasoning and specific examples
- Coherence must feel natural, not structural — penalise formulaic essay templates
- Vocabulary must show range AND precision; near-synonyms with subtle meaning differences matter
- Grammar: complex structures should be controlled consistently; errors should be rare

Key differentiators from Band 7.0:
- Argument sophistication: nuanced position, acknowledgement of counter-arguments
- Cohesion: natural flow between sentences within paragraphs (not just between paragraphs)
- Lexical precision: choosing the exact right word, not just an adequate synonym
- Sentence variety: mixing long and short sentences for effect, not just grammatical correctness

Focus for feedback:
- Flag template phrases that lower the band: "In today's society", "It is undeniable that"
- Identify where arguments could be deepened with a specific example or counter-consideration
- Reward sophisticated hedging language: "arguably", "to some extent", "in many contexts"
- Flag any grammatical structure that appears repeatedly (over-reliance is a Band 7.5 ceiling)
