## Target Band: 7.0

Evaluation priorities at this band:
- Task Response: all parts of the task must be addressed; ideas should be developed with explanation
- Coherence: logical progression of ideas; paragraph unity; less reliance on mechanical transitions
- Vocabulary: some less common vocabulary used appropriately; minor inaccuracies acceptable
- Grammar: a range of complex structures used with generally good control; few errors

Scoring calibration for Band 7.0:
- Task Response: all parts addressed; main ideas extended and supported though some may be under-developed
- Coherence & Cohesion: information is logically organised; a range of cohesive devices; some overuse
- Lexical Resource: uses some less common vocabulary; aware of collocation; some inaccuracies
- Grammatical Range & Accuracy: variety of complex structures; mostly error-free; some minor slips

Focus for feedback:
- Flag over-reliance on simple sentence structures where complex structures would score higher
- Identify vocabulary that is overused or miscollocated
- Flag transitions that are correct but mechanical (e.g., starting every paragraph with "Firstly")
- Reward well-developed paragraphs with example + explanation
