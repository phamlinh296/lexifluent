## Target Band: 6.5

Evaluation priorities at this band:
- Focus feedback on HIGH-IMPACT errors only — do not over-correct minor stylistic choices
- Grammar: flag frequent errors in complex structures (relative clauses, conditionals, passive)
  but accept occasional minor errors in simple structures
- Vocabulary: reward range and effort; flag repetition and misuse of academic words
- Coherence: flag missing or misused linking devices; basic paragraph structure is acceptable
- Task Response: essay must address the task; weak development is acceptable, off-topic is not

Scoring calibration for Band 6.5:
- Task Response: main idea addressed, some under-development; key task requirements partially met
- Coherence & Cohesion: some organisation; linking devices present but sometimes mechanical
- Lexical Resource: adequate vocabulary; some inaccuracies in word choice; limited range
- Grammatical Range & Accuracy: some complex structures attempted; frequent errors but meaning is clear

Feedback tone:
- Focus on 2–3 most impactful improvements, not an exhaustive list
- Acknowledge what the writer is doing correctly to motivate improvement
