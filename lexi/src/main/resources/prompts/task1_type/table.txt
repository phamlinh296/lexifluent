## Task 1 Type: Table

Evaluation focus:
- Must identify the most significant values (highest, lowest, notable comparisons)
- Reward grouping of similar rows/columns before contrasting different ones
- Penalise exhaustive row-by-row listing — selectivity is a sign of a higher-band writer
- Overview must capture the dominant pattern across the whole table
- Reward writers who compare across both rows and columns, not just one dimension

Language to reward:
- Comparative: "the highest figure was recorded for", "in contrast to", "outperformed"
- Qualification: "the only category to show...", "with the exception of"
- Grouping: "In general, X and Y shared similar figures, while Z was notably lower"

Common weaknesses to flag:
- Reporting every cell in the table without selection or comparison
- Missing the overview
- Failing to compare across categories or time periods
