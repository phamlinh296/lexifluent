## Task 1 Type: Mixed / Multiple Charts

Evaluation focus:
- Must describe BOTH charts — penalise essays that focus on only one
- Overview should identify the most significant finding across all charts combined
- Reward writers who find a connection or contrast BETWEEN the two charts
- Describe each chart separately in body paragraphs, then optionally link them
- Higher-band writers will identify a cross-chart relationship (not just describe each separately)

Language to reward:
- Cross-chart linking: "This trend is reflected in the second chart, which shows..."
- Contrast between charts: "While the bar chart suggests X, the line graph indicates Y"
- Combined summary: "Taken together, the data suggests..."

Common weaknesses to flag:
- Spending 90% of the essay on one chart and one sentence on the other
- Failing to identify a relationship between the two datasets
- No overview capturing both charts
