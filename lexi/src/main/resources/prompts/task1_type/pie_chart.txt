## Task 1 Type: Pie Chart

Evaluation focus:
- Must identify the largest and smallest segments in the overview
- Reward writers who group similar categories before describing others
- Penalise mechanical listing: "A was 30%, B was 25%, C was 20%..."
- If comparing two pie charts (same topic, different years), reward descriptions of change
- Do NOT reward conclusions or opinions about the data — Task 1 is descriptive only

Language to reward:
- Proportion language: "accounted for", "made up", "represented", "constituted"
- Comparison: "the largest share", "a quarter of", "almost half", "just over a third"
- Change language (if two charts): "increased its share from X to Y", "saw the greatest rise"

Common weaknesses to flag:
- Listing all segments in order without identifying dominant/minor segments
- Adding personal opinion on the data ("This is surprising because...")
- No overview (immediate Task Response failure)
