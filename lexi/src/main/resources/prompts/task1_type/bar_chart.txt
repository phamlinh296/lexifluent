## Task 1 Type: Bar Chart

Evaluation focus:
- Must include a clear overview identifying the most significant trend or comparison (NOT a conclusion)
- Data selection: reward writers who compare the highest/lowest values and note exceptions
- Penalise essays that list every data point without grouping or comparison
- Reward logical data grouping: e.g., grouping similar categories before contrasting them
- Tense: use past simple for historical data, present simple for current/general data

Language to reward:
- Comparative language: "significantly higher than", "approximately double", "by contrast"
- Approximate language: "roughly", "approximately", "just under/over"
- Trend language: "the most notable feature", "the dominant category"

Common weaknesses to flag:
- No overview paragraph (immediate Task Response failure)
- Merely listing numbers without making comparisons
- Incorrect tense use (e.g., present tense for past data)
