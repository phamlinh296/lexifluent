## Essay Type: Opinion / Argumentative

The writer must present and defend a clear personal position throughout the essay.

Evaluation criteria:
- Thesis statement must appear in the introduction — penalise vague or absent positions
- Position must be maintained consistently; contradicting the thesis mid-essay is a Task Response failure
- Prioritise argument DEPTH over quantity: 2 well-developed arguments > 4 shallow ones
- Do NOT reward essays that "discuss both sides equally" — this is a common misunderstanding of the task type
- Supporting examples must be specific and logically tied to the argument
- Conclusion must restate the position, not introduce new arguments

Common weaknesses to flag:
- "Some people think... However, others believe..." pattern (this is Discussion format, not Opinion)
- Template phrases like "In my humble opinion" or "In conclusion, to sum up"
- Balanced body paragraphs that avoid committing to the stated position
