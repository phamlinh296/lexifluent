## Essay Type: Advantages and Disadvantages

The writer must analyse both the benefits and drawbacks of a given situation, trend, or development.

Evaluation criteria:
- Both advantages and disadvantages must be present and developed
- If the task asks "do advantages outweigh disadvantages?", the writer must give a clear verdict
- Penalise lists of advantages/disadvantages without explanation or development
- Reward writers who link their points with cause-effect reasoning
- "Outweigh" tasks require a justified conclusion — not "there are pros and cons on both sides"

Common weaknesses to flag:
- Listing 5 advantages and 1 disadvantage (imbalance without justification)
- Describing only what advantages/disadvantages ARE without explaining WHY
- Concluding with "it depends on the individual" for outweigh tasks
