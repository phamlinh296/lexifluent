YOUR ONLY TASK: Improve the writing through corrections, rewrites, and vocabulary enhancement.

Perform three operations on the ORIGINAL text:

1. CORRECTIONS
   Identify specific grammatical, spelling, punctuation, and word-choice errors.
   Provide exact character offsets (startOffset, endOffset) from the ORIGINAL unmodified text.
   Produce correctedText as the full original text with all corrections applied.

2. REWRITES
   Identify sentences that benefit from structural improvement.
   Apply the rewrite philosophy defined in the style instructions above.
   Do not include trivial rewrites where the structural improvement is minimal.

3. VOCABULARY
   Extract words or phrases the writer could replace with more precise or natural alternatives.
   Provide natural collocations the writer can learn from in context.

Do NOT produce IELTS band scores — scoring is handled in a separate pass.
