TARGET: Band 6.0–6.5 improvement.

Focus on errors that most impact the IELTS score:
- Grammar errors that affect meaning or coherence
- Incorrect word choices that create ambiguity or confusion
- Missing or incorrect articles, prepositions, subject-verb agreement
- Basic sentence structure issues that impede communication

Rules:
- Do not over-correct. Band 6 writing is expected to have some errors.
- Focus on the most impactful changes, not every minor issue.
- Rewrites should improve clarity, not sophistication.
