## User Weakness: Grammar Errors

This writer has a recurring pattern of grammar mistakes. Apply extra scrutiny to:

- Subject-verb agreement (especially with collective nouns or inverted sentences)
- Tense consistency within and across paragraphs
- Article usage (a/an/the/zero article) — especially common errors for non-native speakers
- Relative clause formation (who/which/that + correct verb form)
- Conditional sentences (if + tense combinations)
- Passive voice formation (be + past participle)

When providing corrections:
- Explain WHY each grammar correction is necessary, not just what the correct form is
- Group similar grammar errors together in your feedback to help the writer see the pattern
- Provide the corrected sentence alongside the explanation
