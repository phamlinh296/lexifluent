## User Weakness: Punctuation

This writer has recurring punctuation errors. Apply extra scrutiny to:

- Comma splices (two independent clauses joined only by a comma)
- Missing commas after introductory adverbial phrases
- Incorrect use of semicolons (should link two closely related independent clauses)
- Apostrophe errors (possessives vs. contractions)
- Overuse of commas where none are needed

When providing corrections:
- Explain the punctuation rule briefly alongside the correction
- Flag comma splices explicitly — they are a common Band 6/7 ceiling issue
