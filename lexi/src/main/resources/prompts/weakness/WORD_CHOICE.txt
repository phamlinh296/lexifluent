## User Weakness: Word Choice / Vocabulary

This writer frequently selects inaccurate or unnatural vocabulary. Apply extra scrutiny to:

- Words used in the wrong context (false friends, overgeneralisation)
- Collocations that sound unnatural (e.g., "do a decision" instead of "make a decision")
- Overuse of high-frequency words where more precise vocabulary exists
- Academic vocabulary misused or used where it sounds unnatural
- Repetition of the same word/phrase within a paragraph where a synonym would improve range

When providing corrections:
- Suggest the more natural or precise alternative
- Briefly explain the collocation rule or register issue where relevant
- Flag instances of vocabulary repetition and suggest range-expanding alternatives
