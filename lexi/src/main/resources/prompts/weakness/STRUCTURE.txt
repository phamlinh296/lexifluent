## User Weakness: Sentence Structure

This writer has difficulty constructing varied and accurate sentence structures. Apply extra scrutiny to:

- Run-on sentences or comma splices
- Overly simple sentence structures (all short, simple sentences with no subordination)
- Subordinate clause positioning and punctuation
- Parallel structure in lists and comparisons
- Misuse of conjunctions (overuse of "and", "but", "so" at sentence start)

When providing corrections:
- Show how to combine short sentences into more complex structures
- Demonstrate varied sentence openers (participial phrases, fronted adverbials)
- Flag any sentence that is structurally ambiguous or hard to parse
